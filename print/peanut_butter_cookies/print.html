Peanut Butter Cookies

dessert cookies baking

Servings

16 Servings

Prep time

195 Minutes

Cooking Time

10 Minutes

Calories

--- Kcal

Steps

Ingredients

  • 112 g unsalted butter
  • 100 g caster sugar
  • 110 g soft dark brown sugar

Step 1

Cream together.

Ingredients

  • 130 g peanut butter
  • 1 large egg

Step 2

Add the peanut butter and egg, then mix in.

Ingredients

  • 160 g plain flour
  • 0.75 tsp bicarb of soda
  • 0.5 tsp baking powder
  • 0.25 tsp salt

Step 3

Mix in a fresh bowl.

Step 4

Mix the dry ingredients into the creamed butter mixture. Place in the fridge for 3 hours.

Step 5

Preheat oven to 190Â°C. Bake for 9-10 minutes.

Notes

  • Chilling: This helps prevent the cookies from spreading out too much in the oven and makes shaping them easier too.
